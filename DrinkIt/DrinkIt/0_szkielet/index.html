About Us

DrinkIt was founded by a passionate group of young students from CODE University who saw an opportunity to make a positive change in Berlin.
Walking the vibrant streets and green parks, we realized that accessible, free water sources were hard to find.
So we came together with a mission: to ensure that everyone in Berlin has access to clean, free water whenever they need it.
Our goal is simple yet impactful—to make hydration accessible while reducing plastic waste.
By combining technology with sustainable practices, we’ve created a platform that maps free water refill points across the city.
Thanks to our partnerships with local businesses and the Refill initiative, we’re proud to offer a network of refill locations and even provide reusable water bottles, sponsored by community-minded businesses.
We believe that a well-hydrated community is a healthier, happier one, and we’re excited to bring DrinkIt to life.
Join us on our journey to create a more sustainable and connected Berlin, one sip at a time.
